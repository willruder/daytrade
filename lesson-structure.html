Market Structure

Market structure describes how price organizes itself over time. Most traders confuse movement with direction — structure explains the difference.

The Three Regimes

Trend: Expansion with higher highs / lower lows.
Range: Balance. Neither side has control.
Transition: The most dangerous and profitable phase.

Non-Obvious Insight

Structure does not break because price crosses a line. It breaks when one side fails to defend a reaction point.

Advanced traders wait for failed continuation — not breakouts — to identify regime change.
