The Core Philosophy

Profitable trading is not about being right more often. It is about structuring trades so that incorrect ideas lose little and correct ideas are allowed to expand.

Price action is not predictive — it is contextual.

Why Most Traders Fail

Late Entries

Most traders enter after confirmation, exactly when risk is worst and reward is capped.

False Certainty

Indicators provide comfort, not edge. Markets reward adaptability, not rigid rules.

Poor Risk Framing

Without asymmetric risk, even good ideas fail long-term.
