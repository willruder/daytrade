Support & Resistance

Levels are areas of memory, not lines of defense.

Why Most Levels Fail

Traders draw too many levels. Institutions care about reactions, not precision.

Strong levels are defined by response, not touches.
The best trades occur when a level fails slowly, not violently.

How to Think About This Concept

The goal is not to memorize patterns. The goal is to recognize when market behavior aligns with a familiar structure and when it deviates from it.

Common Mistake: Treating this concept as a rule instead of a contextual framework.

Professionals focus on conditions, not outcomes. When conditions degrade, they reduce exposure rather than force trades.
